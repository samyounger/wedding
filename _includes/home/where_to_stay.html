Where to stay?

We want to make it as easy as possible for you to get around on the day. As such buses will be booked to transport you from the Melrose Church to Whitslaid, and back in the evening.

We would prefer everyone to book in Melrose to make your transport most convenient to the Church, to Whitslaid and back. If you prefer to use your own transport feel free to book wherever you like.
